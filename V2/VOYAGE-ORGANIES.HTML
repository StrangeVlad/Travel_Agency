Departure Cities: Flights available from Algiers, Oran, and Constantine, ensuring flexibility.

🏨 4-Star Hotel Accommodation

Comfort & Affordability: Stay in spacious rooms with pools, restaurants, and fitness centers at reasonable prices.

Prime Locations: Hotels near Las Ramblas, Sagrada Familia, and the Gothic Quarter for easy sightseeing.

Included Services: Daily housekeeping, 24/7 reception, and free Wi-Fi.

🍽️ Breakfast & Dinner Included

Delicious Meals: Enjoy a variety of Catalan, Spanish, and international dishes, with vegetarian options available.

Cost Savings: Save money by having breakfast and dinner covered, allowing you to enjoy your trip worry-free.

🌊 Beach & Museum Visits

🏖 Beaches:

  • Barceloneta Beach: Vibrant atmosphere with water sports, cafés, and golden sands.
  • Bogatell Beach: A quieter beach, ideal for relaxation and scenic views.

🏛 Museums:

  • Picasso Museum: Home to a vast collection of Picasso’s masterpieces.
  • Barcelona History Museum: Explore the city's rich history from Roman times to the present.
  • Camp Nou Museum: A must-visit for FC Barcelona fans, featuring trophies, memorabilia, and stadium tours.

📅 Daily Itinerary

🛬 Day 1: Arrival & Welcome

  • Arrival in Barcelona and transfer to the hotel.
  • Free time to explore Las Ramblas and nearby attractions.
  • Welcome dinner at a local Spanish restaurant.

🏰 Day 2: Gaudí's Masterpieces

  • Visit Sagrada Familia and Park Güell.
  • Explore Casa Batlló and Casa Milà (La Pedrera).
  • Dinner at a traditional Catalan restaurant.

🛍 Day 3: Shopping & Markets

  • Visit the famous La Boqueria Market for local specialties.
  • Explore the El Corte Inglés department store for souvenirs.
  • Relaxing evening at the beach or in the Gothic Quarter for shopping.

🚢 Day 4: Museums & Beaches

  • Morning visit to the Picasso Museum and Barcelona History Museum.
  • Afternoon at Barceloneta Beach for sunbathing and relaxation.
  • Enjoy dinner at a seaside restaurant with Mediterranean views.

🌆 Day 5: Exploring Montjuïc & Old Town

  • Walk through the Gothic Quarter and visit the Barcelona Cathedral.
  • Take the cable car up to Montjuïc for panoramic views of the city.
  • Explore Montjuïc Castle and the Olympic Stadium.
  • Evening free for leisure or additional sightseeing.

🏝 Day 6: Day Trip to Sitges

  • Full-day excursion to the coastal town of Sitges.
  • Relax on the beautiful beaches and explore the town’s historic center.
  • Lunch at a seaside café and return to Barcelona in the evening.

✈️ Day 7: Departure

  • Free time for last-minute shopping or sightseeing.
  • Transfer to the airport for your departure flight.

Explore the beautiful city of Barcelona with its stunning beaches and Gaudi's architecture.
